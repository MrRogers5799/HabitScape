How to Request Account Deletion

To request deletion of your HabitScape account and all associated data, send an email to the address below with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and include the email address linked to your account.

Request Account Deletion

What Gets Deleted

Upon request, the following data will be permanently deleted within 30 days:

• Your account and login credentials
• Display name and email address
• All habit activities, completion records, streaks, XP, and levels
• Workout templates and session logs
• Any shared workout templates you created

What Is Retained

No personal data is retained after deletion. Aggregated, anonymised data (if any) that cannot be linked back to you may be retained.
